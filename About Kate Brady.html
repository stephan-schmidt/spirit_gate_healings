Kate Brady, M.Ac.Om. holds an MA in Oriental Medicine from the Tai Hsuan Foundation and received a US national board certification in acupuncture and Chinese Herbology. She began her study of acupuncture and Oriental Medicine during a three-year stay in Beijing China where she worked at the Beijing Orthopedic Hospital.  Later she had a  private acupuncture practice in the Washington, DC area. Currently, she is based in Kyoto, Japan.
 
    Kate is also a graduate of the 4-year Barbara Brennan School of Healing, a world leader in the field of energy medicine and personal transformation. She has studied many related fields and is certified in aromatherapy, qigong, Reiki and Toyohari Acupuncture. Kate also has many years experience as an educator and mental health counselor. She is a long-time student of Hatha Yoga and Siddha Yoga Meditation.
 
     Kate offers private Spirit Gate Healing sessions, Windows of Sky spiritual sessions, and holistic workshops on self-healing and empowerment through the human energy field.
